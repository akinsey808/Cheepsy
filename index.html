How Cheepsy works

We build the harness.
You keep the tokens.

Harness engineering is the practice of structuring context around an AI agent so it consistently outperforms what the model alone could do. We do the setup work — skills files, custom agents, hooks, deep-linked documentation — so your agents arrive informed, not confused.

01

Audit your agent setup

We map how your team currently uses AI agents, where context is leaking, and what's costing you the most in wasted tokens and repeated work.

02

Build your harness

We create a structured set of markdown skills, agent configs, and search hooks tailored to your repo and conventions — compatible with every major AI tool your team uses.

03

Agents arrive ready

Your AI agents get project-specific context from day one of every session. Less noise. Fewer tokens. Better output. And when pricing shifts, you're already optimized.
